nacpan beach ii

a laid-back student guide to one of palawan's longest white-sand beaches — the twin beaches of nacpan & calitang.

soft cream sand, coconut palms, turquoise waters, and long sunset views.

view gallery how to go
quick facts
~ 4 km long beach • 45 min drive from el nido town • famous "twin beaches" view point

photo gallery

browse sample shots — perfect for postcard vibes and sunset shoots.

things to do

swim, chill under palms, photography, local seafood, and bajaj tricycle rides to the village.

plan your trip

best time: dry season (nov–may). expect uneven dirt roads near the end — bring shoes for the walk from parking.
