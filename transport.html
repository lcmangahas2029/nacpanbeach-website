how to get to nacpan beach

most visitors travel from el nido town center. below are common options based on local shuttle & travel guides.

1.
van shuttle (recommended for groups)

book via hotels or shuttle services. approx 45 minutes from el nido town. example round-trip rates vary (see shuttle providers for live prices).

2.
motorbike / scooter

rent a moped in el nido (about 45 min ride). road gets rough near the end; ride carefully and carry cash for fuel.

3.
tricycle or local taxi

shorter groups sometimes take tricycles or hire a driver. expect higher per-head price vs shared shuttle.

from manila / puerto princesa

fly to el nido (lio airport) or puerto princesa then transfer to el nido. most major flights to el nido land from manila; the resort and town are accessible by road from lio airport (about 30 min).

tips
  • book shuttle round-trip if you want a worry-free day visit.
  • check luggage space on small vans if travelling with surfboards.
  • arrive early for best beach space and cooler temps.
