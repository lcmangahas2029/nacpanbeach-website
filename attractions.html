nacpan & calitang: twin beaches

the long stretch that locals and visitors call the "twin beaches" is a major draw — four kilometers of sand that meet a shorter inlet (calitang) — a scenic viewpoint on a low hill gives a postcard curve shot perfect for photos.

top spots & activities

twin beaches viewpoint

short climb up a hill between nacpan & calitang. best for morning and late-afternoon photos.

local fishing village

walk toward the small community to see local life and buy fresh seafood. please be respectful and ask before photographing people.

sunset watching

nacpan faces west — sunsets are dramatic and best viewed from the southern end or a beachfront bar.

safety & local tips

  • there are some sections with stronger waves and rip currents — swim where lifeguards or locals advise.
  • bring cash — small stalls and tricycles often don't accept cards.
  • respect local homes, avoid loud parties late at night, and dispose trash properly.
